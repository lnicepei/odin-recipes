Tito's Martini

Description

Enjoy Tito's Handmade Vodka martini-style.

Garnish with one or three olives, but never two.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
  • 1 ounce (or less) dry vermouth
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 small olives

Directions

  1. Just add Tito's Handmade Vodka and dry vermouth to a shaker with ice.
  2. MShake or stir, and strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Garnish with three olives.
